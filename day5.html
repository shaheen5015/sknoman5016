International Study Centre

Where global citizens become future leaders

Begin your world-class education at Royal Holloway International Study Centre

The International Study Centre offers you a pathway to a globally recognised degree from a world-ranked university. Our specialist programmes will help you gain the essential academic and English language skills to succeed at university.
We will be open for your studies (subject to satisfactory completion of our detailed health and safety risk assessment) and supportive staff at the International Study Centre will help you adjust to living and studying in the UK. If you cannot travel to the UK for the start of the first term, you can start your studies online from home.

International Foundation Year

Studying this programme will allow you to progress to the first year of multiple undergraduate degrees at Royal Holloway University London. You can study one of three subject areas, which lead into degrees in areas such as arts, economics and science.
